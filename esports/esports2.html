Key Features

  • We are a leading esports organization dedicated to Indian universities.
  • We organize competitions in six exciting esports games: Valorant, BGMI, CS:GO, CODM, Chess, and Coding.
  • Our mission is to create an inclusive platform for students to showcase their gaming skills.
  • We emphasize teamwork and sportsmanship in our tournaments.
  • The champions of these tournaments earn the opportunity to play alongside professional players in their respective games.
  • Join us as we elevate the esports scene in Indian universities and empower the next generation of gamers.
